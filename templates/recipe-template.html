Soft Turkey Taco Skillet (Weeknight, Gentle)

For tired days: one pan, no fancy steps, cozy flavor.

Prep: 10 min • Cook: 20 min • Makes: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1 cup canned diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed (optional)
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Warm oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion + pepper 4–5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add turkey and cook until no pink remains.
  4. Stir in seasoning + tomatoes. Simmer 6–8 minutes.
  5. Add beans if using. Taste and salt as needed.
  6. Serve in bowls, tortillas, or over rice. Top gently.

Notes (gentle swaps)

  • Higher fiber: add beans or serve over brown rice.
  • Lower effort: use frozen diced peppers/onions.
  • Protein boost: add Greek yogurt as topping.
